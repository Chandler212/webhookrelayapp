Relay webhooks to localhost in under 5 seconds.

Simpler than Ngrok or Cloudflare Tunnel—webhookrelay does one thing only. Pick an app, paste the public webhook URL into the third-party app, run one local listener command. Traffic relays in memory with no server-side archive.
